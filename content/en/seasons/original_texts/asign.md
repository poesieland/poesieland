Under the sign of sun,

You smile... to the world.

The Fall has gone

But you stay. Here.

Tight in the blue night,

The night blue

Like a dream,

Blue

Like a sky,

Unreachable.

Give me your hope

And I'll stay

Among human people,

Close to your way.
